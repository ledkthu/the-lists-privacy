Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

The Lists ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that helps you create, organize, and share lists for everyday life. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email address and username when you create an account
  • Profile data: optional profile picture that you choose to upload
  • List data: the lists, items, and categories you create within the app
  • Collaboration data: information about shared lists and collaborators
  • AI prompts: text you enter when using the Magic List generator feature

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core functionality (creating, saving, and sharing lists)
  • Authenticate your account and keep your data secure
  • Enable collaboration features with other users
  • Generate AI-powered list suggestions via the Magic List feature
  • Send push notifications for list reminders (if you opt in)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase: for authentication, database storage, and file storage. Your account data and list content are stored on Supabase servers.
  • OpenAI: when you use the Magic List feature, your prompt text is sent to OpenAI's API to generate list suggestions. OpenAI processes this data according to their own privacy policy.
  • Expo: for app delivery, updates, and push notifications.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's cloud infrastructure. We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • With collaborators you explicitly invite to your lists
  • With third-party service providers (listed above) solely to operate the app
  • If required by law or to protect our legal rights

6. Guest Mode

You may use the app in guest mode without creating an account. In guest mode, your data is stored locally on your device and is not synced to our servers. Guest data may be lost if you uninstall the app or clear app data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Update or correct your account information via the app's profile settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

8. Children's Privacy

The Lists is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the app. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us
