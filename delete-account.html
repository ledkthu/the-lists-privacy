Delete Your Account

The Lists allows you to request deletion of your account and all associated data. This page explains how to do so and what data will be affected.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion by emailing us directly:

  1. Send an email to ledkthu3009@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your The Lists account

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation once your account has been deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account information (email address, username)
  • Your profile data (profile picture)
  • All lists and items you created
  • Your collaboration data and shared list associations
  • Any AI-generated content from the Magic List feature

Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers. Lists shared with other users will no longer show your name as a collaborator.

Data Retention

After account deletion, no personal data is retained. Anonymized, aggregated usage data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for analytics purposes.
